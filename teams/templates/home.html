What is the Hult Prize?

The Hult Prize Foundation transforms how young people envision their own possibillities as leaders of change in the world around them. With a US$ 1,000,000 global statup prize as its anchor activity, the Hult Prize has brought Impact-focused programs, events and trainings to over a million students globally, creating a pathway for youth everywhere to take action to build a better world.

Why you should participate?

1. Certificates for all participants signed by former US President Bill Clinton and Ahmad Ashkar, CEO of Hult Foundation.
2. Mentorship and incubation opportunities from a renowned paned of investors and industry experts.
3. A chance to represent our college in the prestigious Hult Regional Finals held in one of six coutries around the globel.
4. Finally, a chance to enjoy an unparalleled learning experience, while solving some of the greatest challenges that plague humanity.

This Year's Theme

The Hult Prize organization has officially announced this year's problem statement: "Fashion urgently needs a makeover."
Fashion is the world's second most polluting industry with unsustainability and inhuman working conditions. You will need to brainstorm a game changing social venture that aligns with U.N Sustainable Development goals and positively impacts one or more key stages of the fashion value chain.
The clock is ticking, so pull your socks up and start working on your B-plan.

How to Register?

To participate you must have at least 3 and maximum of 4 members in your team.

If you have decided all the members of your team, then the team leader can register (sign up) and then fill the details of all the other members. Other members will receive the request and can accept, following which they can be added to the team. In case they reject, team leader still has the option to add other members to his/her team.

If you cannot find other team members but wish to participate, then you can register and choose to join other teams by sending them a request. Once your request is accepted, you will be added to that team. Please note that you can only send a request once in 24 hours.
